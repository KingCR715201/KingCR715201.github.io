How Can We Act?

It's important for every neighborhood to contribute to reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Here are simple actions we can take:

  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances.
  • Walk, cycle, or use public transportation to reduce emissions.
  • Plant trees to absorb CO2 and improve air quality.
